# AASTHA

1. **Information Overload**
   The participant experienced an overwhelming amount of information during onboarding, making it difficult to retain and apply knowledge. This resulted in stress and confusion, especially in the early stages.

2. **Lack of Clear Guidance**
   There was no clear direction on what tasks to perform or what to prioritise, which led to confusion and a strong reliance on colleagues for support.

3. **Inconsistent Information**
   The participant often received different answers from different people, leading to uncertainty about what information was correct.

4. **No Centralised System**
   There was no single platform or system to access important information. As a result, the participant relied on memory or asking others repeatedly.

5. **Difficulty Finding Information**
   The participant was often unsure where to find information or who to ask, which slowed down their work and reduced efficiency.
